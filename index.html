About the artist
Daria Shamanova
SHMNV

Daria Shamanova is a contemporary animal painter working in oil. At the center of her practice is an inquiry into individuality, emotional presence, and the inner life of animals.

Through her work, she seeks to portray them not as symbols or objects, but as beings in their own right — inviting the viewer into a deeper dialogue with the living world.

My art is rooted in the belief that every living being has an intrinsic value, regardless of the role people assign to it.

— Daria Shamanova
